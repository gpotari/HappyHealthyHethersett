£6,500 grant secured for Miyawaki pocket forest

18 March 2024

Hethersett Parish Council has been awarded South Norfolk Council funding to create a Miyawaki pocket forest beside the Village Hall, planting 600 native trees with the support of HEAT and CERT volunteers this November.

  • Dense, biodiverse woodland maturing in 10–20 years instead of 100.
  • Mini-forest method delivers 100× faster growth and 40× more carbon storage.
  • Time capsule with local schools will celebrate the community’s journey to Net Zero.
